Deezer - Music streaming app

Role

Visual Designer

Timeframe

3 Weeks

Tools

Figma, Adobe After Effects, Adobe Photoshop

Project Overview

The project goal was to create a visual design strategy, new design language for a brand that included some elements of motion.

I chose to work on Deezer because of the opportunities to create a memorable visual identity without taking away the utility of the app.

Mission

Deezer's mission is to bring audiences and artists together like never before, by embodying the thrill, intimacy, and delight of music discovery.

Deezer is the link between you and the music that moves you. We listen to the beat of your heart and help you find the songs that match it.

Design Goals

My design goals were to

  1. Build a stronger brand presence
  2. Present curated content in a way that feels personal and human
  3. Make music content feel alive

These goals were addressed by designing the following products:

  • Mobile music app
  • Car screen music app
  • Gift cards

Design Process

  • Heuristic evaluation and brand comparisons
  • Planning and strategy
  • Design itertaions and critique
  • Final design delivery

I started my project with an evaluation of the Deezer app and brand against other popular music streaming brands like Spotify and Apple Music. My evaluations focused on brand recognition, human feel and the use of motion. With these in mind, I also evaluated against non-music streaming brands like ASOS
